About KAD e-Tech

KAD e-Tech, an IT service providing company based in India with major focus on Data Entry, Data Processing and Data Reconciliation. It possesses years of experience and has completed number of different data related projects.

Since it’s formation in the year 2010, KAD e-Tech has emerged as one of most innovative data services and back office support providing company in Indore, INDIA. Being headquartered in the center of India, enables us to deal effectively with our clients and allows us to provide them with solutions that are target specific.

KAD e-Tech is a one stop solution for high quality, time bound and cost-effective data entry services like Data Conversion, Data Entry, Forms Processing, Image Tagging, Data Capture, Financial Data Entries, Word Processing, Electronic Document Management & Database Management facilities in India.
